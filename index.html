👋 Google,

We have been working in and around YouTube for nearly 20 years, and one thing we realized recently is that we have never really had the chance to introduce ourselves properly to many of the account teams we could be supporting. So, this page is meant to do exactly that.

Touchstorm is a YouTube agency, and Touchstorm DataLabs is our YouTube insights platform. Together, we help our brand clients turn YouTube into something even more useful than “just” a media channel. Our services and platform deliver actionable strategies, audience insights, competitive intelligence, and result in measureable growth.

Over the years, we have advised global brands in multiple business categories using this simple guiding principle: when brands better understand what is happening on YouTube, they make better decisions on what content to produce, what creators to work with, what media to invest in, and what messaging will resonate with everyone from their prospects and loyal fans and even convert detractors.

That support takes several forms, including options from the service menu shown below, and unfettered access to our data analysis platform.

What makes this especially relevant for Google teams is that Touchstorm is an approved vendor for DIF deployment. That means eligible advertisers can access many of these services through DIF, making it easier for account teams to bring added value to clients in a practical, actionable way. For brands that want a deeper or ongoing solution, we also offer direct platform access.

Our goal is simple: to be a helpful extension of the teams supporting top advertisers on YouTube. Whether the need is insight, strategy, analysis, or execution, we make your recommendations stronger and your clients more successful.

We welcome the opportunity to be a resource for you when the opportunity arises. We look forward to answering any questions and to working together soon.
